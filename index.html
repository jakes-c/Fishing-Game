How To Play

Controls

  • W - Reel up your fishing line
  • S - Lower your fishing hook
  • A - Move hook left
  • D - Move hook right

Gameplay

  • Catch as many fish as possible before time runs out
  • Different fish are worth different points
  • Avoid obstacles - they'll knock fish off your hook!
  • Return to the surface to score your caught fish
  • Each level has unique fish and challenges

Level Progression

Level 1: Shallow waters with common fish

Level 2: Deeper waters with exotic fish and more obstacles

Level 3: The abyss with rare creatures and challenging environment
